About Saylani Welfare Trust

Saylani Welfare Trust is a non-profit organization dedicated to helping the less fortunate by providing essential services and support in various fields including healthcare, education, and food distribution. We are committed to making a positive impact on the lives of those in need.

Health Services

Saylani offers free medical treatment and health services to those who cannot afford them.

Education

We provide educational support and scholarships to deserving students.

Food Distribution

Saylani distributes free food to the needy every day to ensure no one goes hungry.
